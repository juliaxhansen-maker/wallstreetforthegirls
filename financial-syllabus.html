The Financial Syllabus.

Starting in Issue No. 07, every issue closes with a proper deep dive into one of five market pillars — using that week's real story as the walkthrough. Past the highlight reel, into the mechanics, one pillar at a time.

The five pillars

How the syllabus is organized.

Each issue's deep dive is tagged to one of these. Over time, the goal is to work through all five — properly, not just in passing.

Fixed Income

Government and corporate bonds, credit spreads, and the yield curves that track central bank policy.

Equities

Ownership shares in public companies — individual stocks, indexes, earnings, and IPOs.

Foreign Exchange

The global marketplace where currencies trade against one another, and the rate gaps that move capital between them.

Derivatives

Contracts whose value is derived from something else — options, futures, swaps, and volatility instruments.

Commodities & Alternatives

Physical raw materials plus everything off the public exchange — crypto, private equity, real estate, venture capital.
